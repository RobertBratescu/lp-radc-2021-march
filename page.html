Audit Proposal Templates:
GUIDE – New proposal template for SME’s
It is time to stand out. We all need to get into a marketing frame of mind if we want to show how our firm is better than the rest.

To whom it is addressed:

Managers and above (optional: PM’s, when they are part of the proposing process)

When to be used:

  1. When preparing proposals for our local SME targets;
  2. Including situations when a referral from another DTT office is received, LOCAL MANAGEMENT is the decision factor and the selection process is done LOCALLY, based on the proposals.

Why to be used:

Advantages for targets:

  • Contains specific information as a result of past interaction(s) with them. They see that we’ve been listening to them and we are following up on that discussion;
  • A simplified structure, which is focused on three main captions: team, focus areas and fees. They do not have too much time, so it should be easy to browse;
  • Focused on them and their needs. These guys have a big ego: play with that!

Advantages for us:

  • Makes us more visible, as individual professionals;
  • Improves our business language and BD skills;
  • Helps us in making and implementing our own-defined “targeting” approach;
  • Increases our business awareness.
