Deloitte Success Stories is our attempt to lead by the power of example. We selected a total of 12 professionals, covering all of our functions, to tell the story of how opportunities turned into engagements. Most often, the facts of winning a client are there, but the connection between the dots is missing. This is why the project aims to cross analyze the ways in which teams have succeeded despite adversity and to let the project leaders walk us through the winning formulas that they themselves have successfully applied.

Deloitte Success Stories takes the form of interviews - a dialogue between professionals that look for the lessons learned while serving clients with distinction. Each of the 12 interviews will be shared weekly, and will be aggregated below. You can always revisit each of the interviews while looking forward to the next one.

Please be mindful that the information shared within the videos is confidential, and may be used only as a means of understanding how knowledge and expertise come together in better serving our clients for the future.
